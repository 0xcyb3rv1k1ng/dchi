    Frequently Asked Questions

    Here are some frequently asked questions that may be helpful.

    • Why should I get a home inspection for a home I am buying?

      Purchasing a home can be a large investment. A pre-purchase inspection can help put your mind at ease by allowing you to be aware of any existing or potential issues if they exist.

    • Should I get a home inspection if I am selling my home?

      Absolutely. Performing a pre-listing inspection can uncover any problems that need to be addressed or fixed before you sell. This can allow you to receive the best price for your home knowing that any issues have been corrected.

    • What are the benefits of a home inspection?

      One of the greatest benefits of a home inspection is learning the condition of the home. This will tell if there are any major issues or repairs that are necessary for the home. Knowing that the home is in good condition, or exactly what repairs need to be made can give peace of mind.

    • Can a home fail the inspection?

      A home does not pass or fail an inspection. Rather the condition of the home is what is realized. This will also reveal if any repairs will be necessary.

    • Should I be present for the home inspection?

      It is not necessary to be present for the home inspection, but it can be very helpful to be there. This will allow the home inspector to answer any questions or explain any concerns you may have.
